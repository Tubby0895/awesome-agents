# Product Agents

Agents for specification writing, prioritization, and launch planning.

- [feedback-prioritizer](./feedback-prioritizer/): Ranks feedback themes and product opportunities.
- [idea-validator-agent](./idea-validator-agent/): Pressure-tests early product ideas against user pain, feasibility, and strategic fit.
- [launch-planner](./launch-planner/): Builds cross-functional launch plans and readiness checklists.
- [product-spec-writer](./product-spec-writer/): Turns ideas and requirements into implementation-friendly specs.
